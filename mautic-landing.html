Frequently asked questions

How advanced is this course? Do I need prior ML experience?

A working knowledge of Python and foundational ML concepts is helpful, but every module includes refreshers so motivated beginners can follow along.

What tools and libraries are used?

You will work with Python, scikit-learn, PyTorch, distributed data pipelines, experiment tracking tools, CI/CD, and cloud-native deployment patterns.

Does the course cover real-world deployment and monitoring?

Yes. You will containerize services, automate deployments, instrument monitoring, and build runbooks for on-call and retraining workflows.

Can I follow the course without expensive infrastructure?

Absolutely. Each step includes resource-conscious alternatives (serverless, managed notebooks, open-source orchestrators) so you can learn on a laptop or cloud credits.
